General Inquiries

All general inquiries can be found on Access Nova Scotia's website. If you still cannot find your inquiry after searching their page and our page, then please feel free to click here to call us or email us and we will respond to your question.

Requirements For Signing Up

Before signing up for a program, it is required that you complete the written test given by Access Nova Scotia and have obtained a Learner's License. To earn A Learner's License, there are multiple stages to go through which are all listed here . Read carefully all the instructions to apply for the Learner's License. There is a free textbook available here on their website. Read through these chapters before attempting the written test.
