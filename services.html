Services

Full Course

Cost: $640 + tax

Includes:

25 hours of in-class lessons
10 hours of in-car lessons in KDS car
Pick and drop at the student's residence during the road lessons
Course completion certificate is issued by the KDS at the end of the course
Note: This certificate is a requirement for the Graduated Drivers Licensing Program

Road Test

Cost: $160 + tax

Includes:

2 hours of road lesson prior to the test in KDS car
1 hour for documentation and road test
Pick and drop at the student's residence during the road lessons
Course completion certificate is NOT issued

Individual Driving Lesson For those who are not in the full course

Cost: $160 + tax

Includes:

2 hours of road lesson prior to the test in KDS car
Pick and drop at the student's residence during the road lessons
Course completion certificate is NOT issued
